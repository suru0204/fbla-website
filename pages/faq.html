Frequently Asked Questions

For Students

Go to the Job Listings page, find a job you're interested in, and click "Apply Now". Fill in your details and upload your resume before submitting your application.

Yes! You are allowed to apply to multiple jobs that match your skills and interests. However, make sure you only apply to jobs that you are genuinely interested in.

Employers review applications and update your status. You can check your application status on the My Job Status page. You will see whether your application is Approved, Rejected, or In Progress.

Your resume should include:

  • Your **full name and contact details**
  • Your **education background**
  • Any **relevant work experience** or internships
  • Skills related to the job you're applying for
  • Any **extracurricular activities** or certifications

Keep it **clear, concise, and professional**.

For Employers

Go to the Post a Job page, fill in the job details (title, description, company name, etc.), and submit it for approval. Once approved by an admin, your job listing will be visible to students.

Log into your employer account and navigate to the Received Applications page. There, you will see a list of students who applied for your job postings. You can **approve or reject** applications as needed.

Yes! If your job listing is filled or no longer available, you can remove it from the Manage Job Listings page.

Once you approve a student's application, you can use the **email provided in their application** to contact them and schedule an interview.
